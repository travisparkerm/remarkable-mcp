Six personalities. Your notes.

Same notes, six perspectives. Find the voice that makes you think.

The Logbook

"Just the facts"

Crisp, efficient recap. No editorializing. Like a personal assistant reading back your record.

The Analyst

"Here's what stands out"

Identifies the top 2–3 themes and explains why they seem significant. Structured and observant.

The Coach

"Here's what I think is really going on"

Reads between the lines. Asks rhetorical questions that make you think. Direct but empathetic.

The Connector

"Here's how this links to the bigger picture"

Finds recurring threads across time. Spots patterns you'd miss reading note by note.

The Creative

"Here's what's interesting"

Finds the most surprising idea and runs with it. Curious, playful, energetic.

The Editor

"Here's your story"

Shapes your notes into a narrative arc with a beginning, middle, and end. Warm and literary.

Bring your own notebook

However you write, Playback can read it.

reMarkable Tablet

Connect your reMarkable tablet and your notes sync automatically. Browse your library, pick folders or notebooks, and generate episodes from any time window — last day, last week, last month, or all time.

Photo Upload

Snap photos of your handwritten pages. Upload to your personal photo library. OCR converts your handwriting to text automatically — and it's cached, so you never pay for the same page twice. Organize photos into albums and generate episodes whenever you're ready.
